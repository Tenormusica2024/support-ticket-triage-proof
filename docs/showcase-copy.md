## Work title

Support Ticket Triage Proof

## Short description

A sample-first proof that classifies support-ticket-like inputs into urgent, needs-reply, backlog, blocked, and no-action groups. Items that would require an external ticket update are not executed automatically; they are routed into a confirmation queue for human review.

## Safety note

This proof runs only on synthetic sample data. It does not connect to a support portal, CRM, email, task-management, or other external service. It demonstrates support intake decision support and confirmation queueing only.

## Do not overclaim

- Do not describe it as an autonomous ticket updater.
- Do not imply live support portal integration.
- Do not imply it uses private customer data.
- Do not present it as a production helpdesk.
